The Basics

What is Realmheart?

"The living heart of your world."

Realmheart is a world-state engine that runs quietly behind your AI Dungeon adventures — tracking your money, your inventory, the passage of time, the weather, and the rhythm of the world around you, all without you having to lift a finger.

Think of it as the heartbeat of your story. While you focus on the adventure, Realmheart keeps everything else honest: your wallet updates when you spend or earn, your bag reflects what you've picked up or lost, bills come due, seasons change, holidays arrive, and the clock keeps ticking — even when the AI forgets to mention it.

It works by watching the story as it unfolds. Say you picked up a sword, and it's in your inventory. Say a merchant paid you fifty gold, and your wallet reflects it. Mention that a week has passed, and the calendar moves forward with you. No commands required — just play, and Realmheart keeps up.

And when you do want to take the wheel, everything is right there in your story cards — fully readable, fully editable, and built to be changed. Add a bill, adjust the weather, set up a new holiday, rename your currency, or reshape the whole economy to fit your world. Realmheart adapts to Fantasy, Modern, or Sci-Fi settings out of the box, and every card is yours to shape further.

At its heart, Realmheart exists so your world feels real — consistent, alive, and remembered, turn after turn.

⚠️ Known Preset-Switch Behavior (mid-scenario)
Switching Preset on Configure Realmheart currently does regenerate: Bills, Income, Prices & Goods, and Holidays.
It does not regenerate Seasons/Weather — switching leaves the old preset's weather table active under the new preset's label. This is a known bug queued for a future fix.
Starting Balance and Starting Inventory only apply once, at the very first turn of a new scenario — switching preset mid-game never touches your current wallet or bag contents.
